The Mechanical Essentials are easy once you got the hang of it

Presuming that you're a newcomer and not planning on going too indepth into characterization, backstory, motivations, fears, secrets, flaws etc. The first thing you should when creating a character is choosing Class, Race, and Background. And within each of those choices, you have options like subclasses, equipment choices and proficiencies. So long as you choose all 6 of those, roll for your stats and HP, and get an OK with your DM You're document
